Child Safety Standards

Last updated: June 6, 2026

HeadCount has a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

1. Age restriction

HeadCount is strictly intended for adults aged 18 and over. Users under 18 are not permitted to create an account or use the app. We do not knowingly allow minors to access or use HeadCount.

2. Prohibited content

The following content is strictly prohibited on HeadCount:

  • Any sexual content involving minors.
  • Any material that exploits, abuses, or endangers children.
  • Any content that facilitates or promotes child sexual abuse or exploitation.

Violations will result in immediate account termination and will be reported to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant law enforcement authorities.

3. Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior on HeadCount that you believe involves child sexual abuse or exploitation, report it immediately to us at mitchell@ctrlaltorion.com.

You can also report CSAE content directly to:

  • NCMEC CyberTipline
  • Internet Watch Foundation (IWF)

4. Enforcement

We take all reports seriously. Upon receiving a report of potential CSAE content, we will:

  • Immediately investigate the report.
  • Remove any violating content without delay.
  • Terminate the accounts of any users involved.
  • Report the incident to NCMEC and law enforcement as required by law.

5. Contact

For child safety concerns, contact us at mitchell@ctrlaltorion.com. We respond to all safety-related reports promptly.
